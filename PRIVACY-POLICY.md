# Privacy Policy — CaloriX

**Effective date:** April 19, 2026
**App name:** CaloriX
**Developer:** Bimal Kumar Jhariat
**Contact:** bimal.1038@gmail.com

CaloriX ("we", "our", "the app") is a calorie, protein and general health tracker. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

---

## 1. Data we collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on a server.**

When you use CaloriX, the following information stays **only on your device**, inside your phone's local browser/app storage (Android WebView `localStorage`):

- Your name, age, gender, height, weight, activity level, and fitness goal (entered during onboarding).
- Your daily food logs, water intake, mood, and 30-day history.
- Your preferred theme (light/dark).

If you uninstall the app or clear app data, all of the above is permanently erased. We never see it.

---

## 2. Data we do not collect

We do **not** collect, access, or transmit:

- Your contacts, photos, files, location, or microphone.
- Your Google/Apple account details.
- Your IP address or device identifiers (beyond what the OS and advertising SDKs handle automatically — see section 4).

We have **no backend server**, no cloud database, and no analytics service.

---

## 3. Permissions the app requests

- **Internet** — only to display ads (AdMob) and optionally fetch food information (future versions).
- **Camera** (optional, only if you enable it) — used purely on-device to scan food. No photos are uploaded or stored.

You can revoke any permission at any time in your phone's Settings → Apps → CaloriX → Permissions.

---

## 4. Advertising

CaloriX shows banner and interstitial ads served by **Google AdMob** to keep the app free. AdMob may collect standard advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) in accordance with Google's own policies. Google's advertising privacy practices are available at:

- https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
- https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your phone's Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

We do not sell ad data. We do not share any of your in-app data (food logs, profile, etc.) with AdMob or any third party.

---

## 5. Children's privacy

CaloriX is intended for users **18 years and older**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we will help you clear any device data.

---

## 6. Medical disclaimer

CaloriX is for general informational and wellness tracking purposes only. It is **not** a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before changing your diet, starting an exercise program, or making decisions related to a medical condition.

---
